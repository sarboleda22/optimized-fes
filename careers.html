Careers

Fairbanks Energy Services has been providing energy conservation services for commercial and industrial clients throughout the country for over 30 years. Our firm has expertise in developing efficiency solutions in a wide range of facility types, including office buildings, biotech facilities, hospitals, manufacturing facilities and data centers. Our solutions address lighting, HVAC, controls, cooling systems and heating systems.

Fairbanks Energy has experienced rapid growth due to the high demand for turnkey energy solutions. Our increase in growth drove us to open a fifth location within three years of establishment. Adam and Ross Fairbanks founded Fairbanks Energy Services in 2014, with their father Peter serving as President and advisor. Using their experience forming, running, and selling a similar company, the family built a strong start to Fairbanks Energy Services. As a family run business, Fairbanks Energy marries the fast-paced professional environment with a fun collaborative culture.
