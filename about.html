About Fairbanks Energy

Fairbanks Energy Services is a full-service design/build energy conservation firm dedicated to providing cost-effective retrofit solutions for our clients. Our comprehensive approach and deep knowledge of federal, state and municipal incentive programs allows us to identify, develop and install solutions that maximize savings while minimizing capital outlay.

With over thirty years of experience in the energy conservation industry, our team has the skills to understand not only what is possible but create energy-saving solutions that also are aligned with the comfort, aesthetics, and budgetary needs of clients and their employees. Our services include:


  •   Lighting & Controls
  •   HVAC & Mechanical Retrofits
  •   Comprehensive Treatment
  •   Building Management Systems
  •   Data Center Optimization
  •   Internet of Things
  •   Financing
  •   Energy Audit
